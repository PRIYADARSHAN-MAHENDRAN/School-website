About us

          Our academics is based on the philosopy of teaching that can be related to real life and meets the standards of twenty first century skills. The school follows the Equitable Syllabus to prepare the students for the board examinations. It also provides a holistic program of co-curricular activities like yoga, chess, triway, dance, karate, abacus and drawing

The martial art helps to learn defence techniques in order to face any crisis boldly

Students learn various yoga asanas regularly to maintain their physical and mental health.

Students are trained to dance Bharatham, Folk, Kathak and Peacock dance, etc

Regular Physical training, massdrill and games like kho-kho, volleyball, skating, Shuttle-cock, Table-tennis.
